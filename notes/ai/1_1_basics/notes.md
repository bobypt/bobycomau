# Notes: AI Basics – A world of agents working for you

## Article goal

- Paint a vivid picture of everyday life and work with AI agents:
  - Enterprises: fleets of narrow, safe, production agents.
  - Small businesses: a handful of high-leverage agents.
  - Personal life: multiple “micro agents” that know you well.
- Set up mental model of agents (goal, observe, act, loop) without too much technical detail.
- Prepare readers for later deep dives on architecture, safety, deployment, and observability.

## Structure (current version)

1. Opening story: Monday morning, agents have already done work for you.
2. Definition: what an AI agent really is (goal, tools, perception, action, loop).
3. Agents for enterprises – “team of narrow specialists”.
4. Agents for small businesses – force multipliers.
5. Multiple personal agents – your own crew.
6. From vision to reality – steps to get started and link to rest of series.
